International Conference on Recent Advancements in Computational Intelligence and
Sustainable Engineering (RACISE-2024)

The conference will cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to sustainable manufacturing, application of ML in machining optimization, mechanical design, material engineering, mechatronics, robotics, automation systems, signal processing, communications, and electronics instrumentation.

Event Date: November 19th and 20th, 2024

About Conference

This conference serves as an unparalleled platform, uniting researchers, academicians, scientists, practicing engineers, and industry professionals from around the world to discuss sustainable development and recent advancements in Computational Intelligence and its various application in the field of engineering and technology. In recent years, there has been a significant increase in interest in the intersection of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and the Internet of Things (IoT) with sustainable development. Computational Intelligence is essential to the creation and automation of flexible information processing and services that mimic human abilities including sensing, understanding, learning, recognizing, and thinking. Intelligent and sustainable engineering solutions require ongoing innovations, research, and development to improve computational performance across the domain, analyze societal and global concerns more effectively, and generate sustainable engineering solutions. This dynamic platform target to unites visionaries, researchers, and industry professionals worldwide to explore the application of Computational Intelligence and the Internet of Things with sustainable development. From intelligent systems to additive manufacturing, our conference serves as a vibrant stage for the exhibition and thoughtful discussion of research work in the expansive field of all Engineering disciplines. Rapid breakthroughs, fueled by the demand for high-performance and energy-efficient systems, take center stage as we collectively address the pressing issues of current time under the theme "Technology and Its Promise for Sustainable Development."

About The Institute

The North Eastern Regional Institute of Science and Technology (NERIST) is a unique Institute of its own kind in the country, having unconventional and innovative academic programmes. It is an autonomous institute under MHRD, Govt. of India. It was established in 1983 to produce technical manpower at various levels and to meet the technical requirements for the all-round development of the North Eastern States. NERIST got the status of deemed to be university on 31st May 2005 under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. It offers modular pattern of education in all disciplines of Engineering along with Forestry as an applied science discipline from 10th standard to degree levels. It also offers various postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA and MSc) and doctoral programmes in all branches of engineering, applied science, physics, chemistry, mathematics, humanities and management.

About The Department

Department of Mechanical Engineering came into existence as one of the core branches of engineering and started since the inception of the institute in 1986. The department offers Certificate programme, Diploma, Degree in Mechanical engineering in modular pattern of education having lateral and vertical entry. In addition, two M.Tech. programmes and Ph.D. programmes are started in 2006 onwards. The department is currently run 16 Nos. of experienced as well as young motivated well-qualified faculty members from reputed institutes like IISc, IITs, NITs, JU, IT-BHU, BESU, NERIST etc. and experienced supporting staff to generate human resources to cater the fast growing industrial and academic need of the country at various levels.
